## Provenance / processing

Every sprite is procedurally baked by `scripts/gen-colorme-art.mjs` — an original
`@napi-rs/canvas` bake with seeded value-noise material grain and analytic baked
lighting/AO (the same pipeline as the dice / sortshelf / coin-sort materials).
The pot BODIES are heavily desaturated toward their colour so the surround stays
sensory-calm, while the paint MENISCUS keeps the true vivid colour so the pickable
colours stay clearly distinguishable (per the app's sensory-accessibility rule).
`@napi-rs/canvas` is already installed and pinned in `package.json`; nothing new
is fetched.
