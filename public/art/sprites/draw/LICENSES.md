## Provenance / processing

Every sprite is procedurally baked by `scripts/gen-draw-art.mjs` — an original
`@napi-rs/canvas` bake with seeded value-noise material grain and analytic baked
lighting/AO (the same pipeline as the dice / coin-sort / sortshelf materials).
Colours of the surrounding materials are muted per the app's sensory-accessibility
rule; the crayons themselves keep the drawing colours clear. The build tool's only
dependency (`@napi-rs/canvas`) is installed transiently and is **not** shipped.
