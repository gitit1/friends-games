## Provenance / processing

Every sprite is procedurally baked at 224×224 with a transparent background by
`scripts/gen-dice-art.mjs` — an original `@napi-rs/canvas` bake with seeded
value-noise material grain and analytic baked lighting/AO (the same pipeline as
the coin-sort and bowling materials). Colours are muted pastels per the app's
sensory-accessibility rule. The build tool's only dependency (`@napi-rs/canvas`)
is installed transiently (`npm i --no-save`) and is **not** kept in the app.

The spotted die's FRONT FACE occupies a fixed sub-rectangle of the sprite
(`FX0`/`FY0`/`FS` in the bake script, mirrored by `.die-face` in `src/app.css`),
so the runtime CSS count-glow overlay lands pixel-aligned over the baked pips.
