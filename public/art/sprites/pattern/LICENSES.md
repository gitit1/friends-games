## Provenance / processing

Every sprite is procedurally baked with a transparent background by
`scripts/gen-pattern-art.mjs` — an original `@napi-rs/canvas` bake with seeded
value-noise material grain and analytic baked lighting/AO (the same pipeline as
the dice, coin-sort and bowling materials). Bead hues are muted, desaturated
pastels per the app's sensory-accessibility rule (no neon, no fully-saturated
red). The build tool's only dependency (`@napi-rs/canvas`) is installed
transiently (`npm i --no-save`) and is **not** kept in the app.
