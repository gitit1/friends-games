## Provenance / processing

Every sprite is procedurally baked with a transparent background by
`scripts/gen-who-art.mjs` — an original `@napi-rs/canvas` bake with seeded
value-noise material grain and analytic baked lighting/AO (the same pipeline as
the dice, coin-sort and bowling materials). Colours are muted, sensory-calm
warm-oak + dusty-rose per the app's sensory-accessibility rule. The build tool's
only dependency (`@napi-rs/canvas`) is installed transiently (`npm i --no-save`)
and is **not** kept in the app.
