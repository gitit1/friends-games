## Provenance / processing

Every sprite is procedurally baked with a transparent background by
`scripts/gen-dance-art.mjs` — an original `@napi-rs/canvas` bake with seeded
value-noise material grain and analytic baked lighting/AO (the same pipeline as
the coin-sort / dice / bowling materials). Colours are muted pastels per the
app's sensory-accessibility rule (no neon, no strobe). The build tool's only
dependency (`@napi-rs/canvas`) is installed transiently (`npm i --no-save`) and
is **not** kept in the app.
