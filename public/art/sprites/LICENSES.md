## Provenance / processing

Both PNGs were rendered from Kenney's **CC0 vector** (the sports-pack
`charactersEquipment.svg`) at 256×256 with a transparent background, so they are
crisp at the sizes used in-game rather than upscaled from the pack's tiny 18 px
raster sprites. `basketball.png` was additionally desaturated ~15% and darkened
~2% to match this app's calm/muted colour rule (sensory accessibility). CC0
permits use, modification, and redistribution without attribution.
