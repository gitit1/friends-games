## Provenance / processing

Every asset is procedurally baked by `scripts/gen-drawnum-art.mjs` — an original
`@napi-rs/canvas` bake with seeded value-noise material grain and analytic baked
lighting / AO (the same pipeline as the dice, coin-sort and bowling materials).
Colours are muted pastels per the app's sensory-accessibility rule; the numeral
guide and the filled crayon stay razor-legible. The build tool's only dependency
(`@napi-rs/canvas`) is pinned in `package.json`.
