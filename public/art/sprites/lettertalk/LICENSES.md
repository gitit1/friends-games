## Provenance / processing

Every sprite is procedurally baked with a transparent background by
`scripts/gen-lettertalk-art.mjs` — an original `@napi-rs/canvas` bake with seeded
value-noise material grain and analytic baked lighting/AO (the same pipeline as
the dice and coin-sort materials). Colours are muted, desaturated boho tints per
the app's sensory-accessibility rule (no neon, no jarring saturation).

The block's FRONT FACE occupies a fixed sub-rectangle of the sprite
(`FX0`/`FY0`/`FS` in the bake script, mirrored by the `.lt-block` custom
properties in `src/app.css`), so the runtime lays the razor-crisp CSS Hebrew
**glyph** and the googly eyes exactly over the baked front face. The glyph itself
is never baked — it stays live CSS TEXT for perfect legibility at any size.
