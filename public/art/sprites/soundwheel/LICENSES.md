## Provenance / processing

Every sprite is procedurally baked with a transparent background by
`scripts/gen-soundwheel-art.mjs` — an original `@napi-rs/canvas` bake with seeded
value-noise material grain and analytic baked lighting/AO (the same pipeline as
the dice, coin-sort and bowling materials). Colours are muted pastels per the
app's sensory-accessibility rule (no neon). Total folder size is ~378 KB.

The Hebrew LETTERS are **not** baked — they stay crisp CSS text laid over the
baked buttons (`.sw-glyph` in `src/app.css`) so they are razor-legible and
upright at rest; the vowels are shown only as the baked enamel colour wells.
